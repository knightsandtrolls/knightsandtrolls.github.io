Knights and trolls

Play as a knight in a set of different levels in this arcade platform game.

Goals

  • Find the map to unlock the next level
  • Fight the evil trolls that block your way
  • Collect the life-potions to regenerate health

Commands

  • Use LEFT, UP, and DOWN keys to move aroung and jump
  • Use the 1 key for the main attack
  • Use the 2 for the secondary attack
